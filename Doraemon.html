Doraemon is an anime that originated from Japan in 1979, but since it was first aired in India in 2005, it has made its way into the hearts of millions of kids across the country. The show is based upon its titular character, a cat-type robot from the 22nd century who has travelled back in time to aid a schoolboy, Nobita, who is also the protagonist of the series. Each episode usually involves Nobita getting into one mess or the other and asking Doraemon for a gadget from his fourth-dimensional pocket to resolve his issues.

Doraemon's Pocket

Doraemon's pocket has a multitude of various gadgets used to perform seemingly miraculous feats utilising the technology of the fictional 22nd century. The most popular of these include:

Bamboo Copter
The bamboo copter is a small yellow contraption with a rotor fan attached to it. It attaches to the user's head and allows them to fly in the air.
Time Machine
The time machine is used to travel to any point in time and space imaginable.
Air Cannon
The air cannon is a cylindrical device worn on the arm that shoots powerful blasts of air.
Time Wrapping Cloth
This cloth is used to increase or decrease the age of any object or living being wrapped in it.
Translation Jelly
Once eaten, this jelly can make anyone understand, as well as speak, any language from across the world.
