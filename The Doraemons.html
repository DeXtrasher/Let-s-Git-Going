THE DORAEMONS



The Doraemons is a spin-off from the original Doraemon series. It features Doraemon and the numerous friends he made while in robot school (all similarly designed cat-type robots, but vastly different in terms of their nationality, appearance and personality) across various escapades and adventures. Each one of them also has a Friendship Telecard: a gadget that is proof of the bond of their friendship. All of the Doraemons love to eat Dorayaki/Doracakes, but each prefers to add their own seasoning to it.
